About LocknoxIntel

LocknoxIntel is a premier cybersecurity firm operating out of Mumbai, India, with a presence in Dubai.

Specializing in a wide array of cybersecurity services, we offer expertise in cyber security audits, malware analysis, endpoint security, consultation, digital forensics and investigation, training, vulnerability assessment and penetration testing, identity and access management, intellectual property enforcement, cyber crime investigations, and web application development.

At LocknoxIntel, we are dedicated to fostering secure environments and delivering top-tier security solutions. Our commitment to excellence ensures that we provide our clients with the best-in-class protection against evolving cyber threats.

Services

What we do for your Cyber security solutions

CYBER SECURITY AUDIT

LocknoxIntel conducts comprehensive audits to evaluate the cybersecurity posture of organizations. We assess existing security measures, identify vulnerabilities, and provide actionable recommendations to enhance resilience against cyber threats.

MALWARE ANALYSIS

Our experts analyze malicious software to understand its behavior, origins, and potential impact on systems. Through detailed examination, we develop effective strategies to mitigate malware threats and protect against future attacks.

ENDPOINT SECURITY

LocknoxIntel offers robust endpoint security solutions to safeguard devices such as computers, mobile devices, and servers. Our proactive approach ensures continuous monitoring and protection against evolving threats to endpoint devices.

CONSULTATION

We provide expert guidance and strategic advice on cybersecurity best practices and risk management. Our consultants work closely with clients to develop tailored solutions that align with their business objectives and security requirements.

DIGITAL FORENSIC & INVESTIGATION

LocknoxIntel specializes in forensic analysis and investigation of digital evidence to uncover cyber crimes. Our experienced team conducts thorough investigations, gathering evidence and providing support for legal proceedings.

TRAINING

We offer comprehensive training programs to educate employees and individuals on cybersecurity awareness and best practices. Our interactive sessions equip participants with the knowledge and skills needed to recognize and respond to cyber threats effectively.

INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY ENFORCEMNT

LocknoxIntel assists organizations in protecting their intellectual property rights online. We employ proactive monitoring and enforcement strategies to detect and address instances of infringement, safeguarding valuable intellectual assets.

CYBERCRIME INVESTIGATION

Our team conducts thorough investigations into cyber crimes, working closely with law enforcement agencies. We gather evidence, analyze digital traces, and collaborate with authorities to identify and apprehend cyber criminals.

IDENTITY & ACCESSMANAGEMENT

We provide solutions for managing and securing user identities and controlling access to sensitive data and resources. Our identity and access management solutions help organizations enforce security policies and prevent unauthorized access.
